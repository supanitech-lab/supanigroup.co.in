Meet Panigrahi Sujith

Born and raised in Srikakulam, technology has always been my core passion. Over the years, I noticed that homes and businesses across our locality often struggle to find honest, high-end technical support without traveling to major distant cities. To solve this gap, I founded Supani Technologies right here in our community.

We are dedicated to providing cutting-edge surveillance and infrastructure engineering. Whether protecting your business with smart high-definition CCTV installations or keeping your laptops performing flawlessly, my focus remains entirely on standard precision and uncompromised local reliability.

"My goal is remarkably simple: to deliver sophisticated corporate-grade security and tech systems straight to your doorstep across Pathapatnam and Parlakhemundi with absolute transparency."
